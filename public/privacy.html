Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 11, 2026

La Shoe de Peugh ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect when you visit lashoedepeugh.com or place an order, how we use it, and the choices you have.

Who we are

La Shoe de Peugh is a small business based in Owosso, Michigan, selling our peppermint Shoe & Foot Deodorizing Spray. You can reach us any time at lashoedepeugh@aol.com.

Information we collect

Information you give us

  • Order & contact details — your name, email address, shipping address, and order contents when you buy from us or submit a bulk-order request.
  • Messages — anything you include when you email us or fill out a form.

Payment information

Card payments are processed securely by our payment provider, Clover, on their hosted checkout page. Your full card number is entered on Clover's system, not ours — we never see or store your complete card details. We receive only confirmation of the order and the information needed to fulfill it.

Information collected automatically

Our website stores a simple visitor count in your browser's local storage to display a tally in the footer. This is kept only in your own browser and is not linked to your identity. We do not currently run third-party advertising or analytics trackers on the site. Standard server logs may record basic technical data (such as your browser type and IP address) as part of normal web hosting.

How we use your information

  • To process, fulfill, and ship your orders.
  • To send order confirmations, receipts, and shipping updates.
  • To respond to your questions and bulk-order requests.
  • To keep records for accounting, tax, and legal compliance.
  • To detect and prevent fraud or misuse.

How we share your information

We do not sell your personal information. We share it only with service providers that help us run the store, and only as needed:

  • Clover — to process payments.
  • Shipping carriers (such as USPS) — to deliver your order.
  • Web3Forms — to deliver bulk-order request messages to our email inbox.
  • GitHub Pages & Cloudflare — to host and serve the website and checkout function.
  • Legal / safety — if required by law, or to protect our rights, customers, or the public.

Cookies & local storage

We use minimal browser storage, primarily the local visitor-counter value described above. Our checkout provider may set cookies necessary to process your payment securely. You can clear or block browser storage in your browser settings, though some features may not work as intended.

Data retention

We keep order and contact information for as long as needed to fulfill your order and to meet our legal, accounting, and tax obligations, then delete or anonymize it.

Your choices & rights

You may ask us to access, correct, or delete the personal information we hold about you, or to opt out of non-essential emails. Just email lashoedepeugh@aol.com and we'll respond within a reasonable time. Depending on where you live (for example, California or other U.S. states), you may have additional rights under applicable privacy laws — we honor those requests where they apply.

Children's privacy

Our store is intended for adults. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us information, please contact us and we will delete it.

Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your information, and payments are handled by a PCI-compliant provider. No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, but we work to safeguard your data.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date above reflects the most recent version. Material changes will be posted on this page.

Contact us

Questions about your privacy? Email lashoedepeugh@aol.com or write to us at La Shoe de Peugh, Owosso, MI 48867.
