Refund & Return Policy

Last updated: July 11, 2026

We want you to love your peppermint freshness. If something isn't right, we're here to help.

Our promise

If your order arrives damaged, defective, or incorrect, we'll make it right with a replacement or refund. Just contact us within 7 days of delivery.

Returns of unopened product

Unused, unopened bottles in their original condition may be returned within 7 days of delivery for a refund of the product price. To keep things fair, return shipping is the customer's responsibility unless the return is due to our error.

Opened or used product

Because our spray is a personal-care product, opened or used bottles cannot be returned for hygiene and safety reasons. If your sprayer is defective, contact us and we'll replace it.

Damaged or wrong items

If your order arrives damaged in transit or you received the wrong item, email us within 7 days with your order number and a photo of the issue. We'll arrange a replacement or refund at no extra cost to you.

How to start a return or report a problem

  1. Email lashoedepeugh@aol.com with your order number and a short description (add photos if the item is damaged or defective).
  2. We'll reply with instructions and, if a return is needed, the return address.
  3. Once we receive and inspect the item (or approve your request), we'll process your refund or send a replacement.

Refund timing

Approved refunds are issued to your original payment method, usually within 5–10 business days after we receive the returned item or approve your request. Your bank or card issuer may take additional time to post the credit. Original shipping charges are non-refundable except when the return is due to our error.

Order changes & cancellations

Need to change or cancel an order? Email us as soon as possible. We'll do our best to catch it before your order ships, but once an order is on its way it can't be canceled.

Questions

We're a small business and we genuinely want you happy with your purchase. Reach out any time at lashoedepeugh@aol.com.
