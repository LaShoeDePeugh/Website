Shipping Policy

Last updated: July 11, 2026

We ship every order of peppermint freshness from Owosso, Michigan. Here's what to expect.

Where we ship

We currently ship within the United States only. If you'd like to arrange shipping elsewhere, email us at lashoedepeugh@aol.com and we'll do our best to help.

Shipping rates

Shipping is a flat rate per order based on how many bottles you buy — and it drops as you add more. Orders of 5 or more bottles ship FREE.

Bottles per orderShipping
1 bottle$5.95

3–4 bottles$1.95

Your exact shipping total is shown at checkout before you pay.

Processing time

We typically process and pack orders within 1–3 business days. Orders placed on weekends or holidays are processed the next business day.

Delivery time

Once shipped, most U.S. orders arrive within 3–7 business days, depending on your location and the carrier. Delivery times are estimates and are not guaranteed.

Tracking

When available, tracking information will be sent to the email address on your order. If you haven't received an update within a few days of ordering, check your spam folder or email us.

Shipping address

Please double-check your shipping address at checkout. We are not responsible for orders delivered to an incorrect address provided at checkout. If a package is returned to us as undeliverable, we'll contact you to arrange re-shipment (additional shipping may apply).

Lost or delayed packages

If your order is significantly delayed or appears lost in transit, please reach out to lashoedepeugh@aol.com and we'll help track it down and make it right.

Questions

Email lashoedepeugh@aol.com with your order number and we'll be glad to help.
